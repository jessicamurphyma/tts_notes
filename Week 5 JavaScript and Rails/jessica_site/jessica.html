My Bio

My name is Jessica Murphy. I am from originally from the Atlanta, GA area. I moved to Phoenix approximately 10 years ago.

In Atlanta, I worked as a sign language interpreter. I moved to Phoenix because there were opportunities for interpreters. After moving, I quickly became an instructor. A few years later, I decided that my next opportunity would be in disability services. I pursued a master's degree believing that I would work in disabilities services. After graduating, however, I was told that I was overqualified. After one of these occassions, I met a woman from Women Who Code (WOC). She told me that she loves teaching women to code. Those words changed my life. Since that time, I have gone to every meetup, workshop and conference that I could attend.

After my encounter with the developer from WOC, I have taken a few classes. Some of those classes include (but are not limited to):

  • HTML | CSS | Javascript | Ruby (CodeAcademy)
  • Creating Web Pages (Gale Courses)
  • Introduction to CSS3 and HTML5 (Gale Courses)
  • Introduction to JavaScript (Gale Courses)
  • The Complete Web Developer Course (Udemy)

It has been fun working with everyone in our Tech Talent South code immersion class. Let's stay in touch!
